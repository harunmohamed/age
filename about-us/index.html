African Grand Expeditions Limited

African Grand Expeditions Limited (AGE) is a fully licensed Tour Operator providing tours, hotel bookings, airline bookings, special safaris, Incentive and Conference Organizing and travel related services within East Africa.

African Grand Expeditions Limited provides quality and personalized Safaris at the most affordable prices to all discerning travelers.

The success of any African Safari largely depends upon the calibre of your safari consultants and guides. In this respect, we take utmost care  in selecting responsible, qualified people who are capable of providing a cheerful, caring and attentive services to our clients.We take pride in ensuring that our services are not only the best but surpasses the expectation of our clients and partners.

All our safari guides are vetted  and qualified to KPSGA ( Kenya Professional Safari Guides Association) with a minimum qualification being Bronze Level. Besides, all our staff and guides have undertaken rigorous training and refresher courses and are experts in their chosen fields. Some of our staff are bilingual, and all have in - depth knowledge of East Africa - its history, Geography, culture, customs and attractions.

We are indeed your Trusted and  True African Safari Partner!

The team at African Grand Expeditions Limited invites you to visit Africa and experience our part of the continent for the adventure it offers.
This is the place to come for safari. The East Africa Region is bestowed with lots of wildlife including a variety of birds and mammals, and also exhibits a cultural diversity and natural habitat like no other. It’s beautiful scenery with breathtaking landscapes and white sandy beaches is unmatched, and we are enthusiastic about our growing list of exciting and personalized safari packages.

At African Grand Expeditions, we not only present travelers’ with the standard safari programs, but also are keen and always go the extra mile to understand and meet the needs of the traveler, hence offering personalized services all through. We are able to accommodate different forms of interests, time frame as well as valid budgets.


Important to mention is that we have daily departures for all our safaris and these operate on private basis, with a minimum of 2 persons booking. We have English and language speaking driver/guides – French and German upon request. Get in touch with us and let us work together in creating your next adventure!
Choose from our well selected range of East African Safaris.

Road Safaris (with the option of using either a custom safari minibus or 4×4 land cruiser), Kenya flying safaris, Tanzania camping and Tanzania lodge safaris, Uganda gorilla safaris as well as Rwanda gorilla safaris. We also arrange for climbing excursions to Mt Kenya and Mt Kilimanjaro, using all the different scenic routes.
After all the activity in the parks and if time allows, it is worthwhile to let go and relax on a white sandy palm-fringed beach beside the clear blue waters of the Indian Ocean. Allow us to arrange your beach holidays.
