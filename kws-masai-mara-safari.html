Masai Mara Safari

5 Days Safari Highlights:
Nairobi, Masai Mara, Nakuru, Baringo, Bogoria
Safari Itinerary:

Day 1: Nairobi - Masai Mara
After breakfast at your hotel, you will make and early departure from Nairobi for a journey south along the floor of the Great Rift Valley. Arrive in time for a delicious lunch. The afternoon will be spent game viewing; the best time for game viewing is late afternoon, when the heat of the day encourages the animals out to eat. Dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Game Drives in Masai Mara
Masai Mara Full day spent in search of the elephant, rhino, buffalo, lion, leopard and other plains game. There are option activities like an early morning balloon flight or safari which gives you the chance to get closer to the animals in their natural environment. Or you may decide to take a nature walk or visit to a nearby Masai Village.

Day 3: Masai Mara to Nakuru
After breakfast leave the lodge and depart for Nakuru. Lunch is served en-route. Lake Nakuru is renowned for its large variety birds. The park has also been enlarged, partly to set up a sanctuary for black rhino. Enjoy sundowners overlooking the lake followed by dinner.

Day 4: Nakuru to Baringo
Today the morning will be spent viewing game at Lake Nakuru and in the afternoon proceed to Lake Baringo. There is also the option of a boat trip to view the many birds which inhabit this lake. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 5: Baringo to Nairobi via Bogoria
After breakfast drive to Lake Bogoria. This National Park is well known for its hot springs and geysers, which erupt sporadically and violently throughout the area, but many visitors enjoy what feels like a natural spa. On the southern end of the lake may find gazelles, leopards, and buffaloes. The most popular attractions are the thousands of flamingos that gather on the lake. Depart after a picnic lunch for Nairobi via Nakuru.
